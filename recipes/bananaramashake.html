Bananarama Shake Recipe

Shake that drama off

This Banana Milk Shake can ease up your melodramatic break up. Every sip of this creamy milky shake will take that drama out of your system.

Ingredients

  • 2 Bananas
  • 2 tbsp of Peanut Butter
  • 3 tbsp Honey
  • 1 pc of Snickers chopped
  • 1 cup of Milk or Plant base milk whatever you like
  • 1 cup Crushed Ice
  • 1 scoop of Vanilla Ice cream

Method

Take your blender and just dump everything in there. Less work right? You don't have time to do all the preparations because you are still mourning from the break up! Enjoy!
