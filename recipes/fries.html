Parma Fries Recipe

Simple Fries with an Extra

A very simple way to have a fries with a little bit of elegance

Ingredients

  • Frozen Fries, take your fave one
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley

Method

You are probably tired for crying your heart out when you reach this recipe. So, we will make it simple for you. Re-heat your oven to 200c after 30 mins throw the frozen fries in there and sprinkle the parmesan cheese on top. When cooked sprinkle with parsley so it looks edible.
