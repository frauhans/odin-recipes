The Juciest Burger

This Burger is so juicy that it is more juicier than the gossip about your ex's new GF

Ingredients

  • 500 grams Ground Beef 30% fat and 70% lean
  • 1/2 cup Crushed Medium Onion
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tbsp Cayenne Powder
  • Ofcourse, salt and pepper

Method

Again, you dont have the time! Just put everything in a bowl and just mix it with your bare hand. crush it like you crushing your ex :D and if its well mixed, cook it in an iron cast skillet. cook it into your liking!
